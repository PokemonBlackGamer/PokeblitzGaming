Shiny hunting guide Pokédex Rental teams

How to get the Oval Charm.

First, you need to complete Pokédex. Thern, you need to fly to Circhester. Go to the right of the Pokécenter and enter Hotel Ionia. Go up the elavator Come to the left and enter the first door and battle the police officer. He will give you the Oval Charm.

How to get the shiny Charm.

Next go all the way down the hall and talk to the doctor he will give you the shiny charm this increases the odds of finding a shiny Pokémon.

Get a foreign Ditto.

You need to get a foreign Ditto for this method, so If you have a friend playing the game in another language then have them trade you a Ditto if not try to find one on twitch.tv

Time to breed.

Go to the daycare in Route 5 or the wild area. Put your Ditto and the pokemon you want to shiny hunt in the daycare by talking to the lady. Then walk around/ride on your bike until the lady crosses her arms when she does grab the egg. If it is not shiny keep walking around and getting another egg until you find a shiny.
